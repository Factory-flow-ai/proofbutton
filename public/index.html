Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ProofButton

How does it work without batteries? +

ProofButton uses kinetic energy harvesting technology. When you press the button, the mechanical energy is converted directly into electrical power that sends a secure wireless signal. No batteries, no charging, no excuses — ever. The button will work for 100,000+ presses over its lifetime.

What happens if I lose the button? +

The ProofButton hub can support multiple buttons. You can purchase additional buttons separately at retail price. Each button pairs to the hub with a unique ID, so you can replace or add buttons as needed.

Does it work with my smart home system? +

ProofButton works with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Home Assistant via our wireless hub. The hub connects to your WiFi and can trigger smart home routines, send notifications, or log actions to your mobile app. No cloud required — everything stays private on your local network.

What's the range of the wireless signal? +

ProofButton uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) with a range of 150+ feet in open space. The signal works through walls and obstacles, making it reliable throughout a typical home. The hub can relay signals to your WiFi network for remote notifications.

Can I customize what the button does? +

Yes! Through the mobile app, you can configure each button to send SMS notifications, trigger Alexa routines, control smart home devices, log to a spreadsheet, or send webhooks to any service. The button face itself is custom printable — print your own design or use our templates.

We're launching on Kickstarter [Launch Date]. If you back the campaign, early bird backers can expect delivery in [Estimated Delivery]. We have manufacturing partners in place and our tooling is complete, so we're ready to produce at scale.

Is my data private and secure? +

Absolutely. ProofButton data stays on your local hub — no cloud storage, no tracking, no ads. All communication is encrypted. Your family's press history and automations are private and under your control. We never sell or share data.

What if I have technical problems? +

We offer lifetime support via email, phone, and a community forum. Our mobile app includes troubleshooting guides, and we maintain regular firmware updates to fix bugs and add features. If a button is defective, we'll replace it free of charge.
