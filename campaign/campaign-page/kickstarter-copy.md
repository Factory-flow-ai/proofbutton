## How It Works

### **Three Steps to Accountability**

**Step 1: Label Your Button**
Pick what you need to track. "Medicine." "Chickens." "Dishes." "Practice Piano." "Gym." "Homework." "Bedtime." Download our custom label templates, print them, or engrave them. Peel off the adhesive backing. Stick the label on.

**Step 2: Stick It Anywhere**
Mount it wherever the action happens. Bathroom mirror for medicine. Chicken coop for feeding. Kitchen fridge for toilet paper reminders. Entryway for "shoes off." Use the peel-and-stick adhesive or optional screw mount. It's weatherproof, so outdoors works too.

**Step 3: Press It. Just Press It.**
When the task is done, press the button. That's it. One satisfying *click*. The button generates its own power from that press. The signal travels via Bluetooth to your ProofButton hub (placed anywhere WiFi reaches). The hub translates the signal and:

- **Sends a text** to whoever you designated (or you can ignore texts and just log it)
- **Makes a home announcement** ("Linkin took his medicine!")
- **Toggles smart lights** (Chicken coop light turns on automatically)
- **Updates automations** (toilet paper added to Amazon list, email sent to a caregiver)
- **Logs a timestamp** (Proof, forever, visible in the app or Home Assistant dashboard)

**The hub does the heavy lifting.** One hub controls up to 32 buttons. That tiny white box does all the WiFi translation, the Alexa integration, the logging. Plug it in once. Set it up (2 minutes with the app or Home Assistant). Forget about it.

**No subscriptions. No cloud dependency (unless you want it).** Your buttons work even if the internet goes down. The hub stores the timestamps locally. When WiFi comes back, it syncs.
