## Risk Mitigation

**Potential Delays & Backup Plans:**

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Factory delay (1-2 weeks) | Fulfillment pushed | Communicate proactively, offer swag as apology |
| KS page approval delay | Campaign launch delayed | Have backup KS launch date ready (1 week later) |
| Lower than expected pledges | Underfunded stretch goals | Have "minimum viable" feature set ready |
| Major tech issue on launch day | Campaign doesn't launch | Have pre-recorded video message ready, social posts queued, email list informed |
| Negative press/review | Damage to credibility | Respond professionally, address concerns, share testimonials |
| Supply chain issue (component shortage) | Production impacted | Negotiate factory alternative suppliers, increase timeline buffer |
| High return rate post-fulfillment | Reputation damage | QC rigorously, have excellent customer support ready, issue refunds quickly |
