## Campaign Strategy Summary

**Budget Allocation Recommendation:**
- Concept 1 (Medicine): 25% — Largest addressable audience, highest emotional resonance
- Concept 2 (TP Problem): 20% — High engagement rate, viral potential, humor-driven shares
- Concept 3 (Tech Specs): 20% — Tech-forward audience, conversion-focused
- Concept 4 (Family Story): 20% — Brand trust and authenticity, founder narrative
- Concept 5 (Airbnb): 15% — Niche but high-value hosts and hospitality pros

**Success Metrics by Concept:**
- Concepts 1 & 4: Target 2-4% CTR, focus on email signups
- Concept 2: Target 3-6% CTR, focus on viral engagement and shares
- Concept 3: Target 2-3% CTR, focus on conversions to Kickstarter
- Concept 5: Target 1-2% CTR, focus on high-value host conversions

**Testing Approach:**
- Launch all five concepts simultaneously in small budget allocations
- Pause lowest performers after 48-72 hours of data
- Scale winners in final week before Kickstarter launch
- A/B test CTAs and primary text within concepts
